Our Story

1999 The Beginning

A Vision Born
in the Hills

The Park Queen was founded in 1999 by the Rajput family with a singular vision: to create a sanctuary in the Himalayan foothills that would match the grandeur of India's most celebrated palaces, while offering the warmth and intimacy of a family home.

What began as a 40-room heritage property has blossomed over two decades into Shimla's most iconic luxury address — 248 rooms, five dining venues, a world-class spa, and an events portfolio that has hosted royalty, heads of state, and discerning travellers from every corner of the globe.

Our philosophy remains unchanged: every guest is royalty. Every stay is a private audience with the extraordinary.

Why Choose Us

The Park Queen Difference

Six pillars that define our commitment to unparalleled excellence.

Bespoke Service

Our dedicated butler team anticipates every need before it is expressed. From personalised pillow menus to curated local experiences, every detail is tailored exclusively for you.

Heritage Architecture

Our colonial-era property is a living monument of history — lovingly restored to preserve its original grandeur while seamlessly incorporating contemporary comforts and technology.

Culinary Mastery

Five dining venues helmed by celebrated chefs offer a gastronomic journey that spans continents — from authentic Himachali cuisine to contemporary European fine dining.

Sustainable Luxury

We believe that true luxury must be responsible. Our green initiatives — from solar energy to zero-waste kitchens — ensure that our footprint on this beautiful landscape remains gentle.

Holistic Wellness

Our Royal Spa draws from centuries-old Ayurvedic wisdom, offering transformative wellness journeys that restore balance to body, mind, and spirit amidst Himalayan serenity.

Unwavering Privacy

Discretion is the hallmark of true luxury. Our entire team is trained to the highest standards of privacy, ensuring that every stay remains entirely confidential and secure.
